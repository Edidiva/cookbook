OKRO SOUP

Okra soup is one of the most common soups in Nigeria as it is eaten by many tribes in Nigeria. its a simple soup to prepare that tastes absolutely delicious which can be paired with different with any swallow like garri , semo, fufu etc. it is made from its edibke green seed pods. Now let's get to it.

Ingredients

Items Measurement
Okra 15-20 okra pods
Goat meat 2kg
Palm oil 50ml
Oziza seeds 10 seeds
Scotch bonnet pepper 6
Cray fish 1 table spoon
Stock fish 0.5kg
Onions 1 ounce
Maggi crayfish 3
Salt To taste

Steps on how to prepare

  • Firstly, wash all your ingredients ie the okra, oziza seeds, pepper, meat and stock fish
  • Chop your okra into tiny bits, grind your scotch bonnet pepper and cut your onions
  • Steam and boil your meat and stock fish
  • Now pour some oil into a dry cooking pot, allow to become hot
  • Add your onions, pepper and oziza seeds. Allow tom simer for some minutes
  • Add your meat and fish stock. Allow to boil for a few minutes
  • Now add your diced okra and cook for 6 minutes
  • Season with seasoning cubes, crayfish add salt to taste
  • Cook for 2 minute and get ready to serve
